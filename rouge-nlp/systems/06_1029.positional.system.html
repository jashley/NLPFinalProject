[1] 1 i have before me an application by applicant nbbd for leave pursuant to order 52, r 15(2) to file and serve a notice of appeal from the judgment of federal magistrate barnes pronounced on 19 january 2006 in which federal magistrate barnes dismissed an application for review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ('the tribunal') affirming the rejection by the respondent's delegate of applicant nbbd's application for a protection visa pursuant to the provisions of the migration act 1958 (cth) ('the act'). [2] since neither a notice of appeal nor an application to extend time for the filing of a notice of appeal was filed within 21 days of 19 january 2006, the applicant must satisfy the court that 'special reasons' exist for the grant of leave to file and serve a notice of appeal.
