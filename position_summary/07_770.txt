(first court hearing) introduction 1 on 20 april 2007 i made an order pursuant to s 411(1) of the corporations act 2001 (cth) ("the act ") that the plaintiff ("apn") convene a meeting ("scheme meeting") of the holders of ordinary shares in apn ("apn shareholders") other than independent news &amp; media (australia) limited ("offeree shareholders") for the purpose of considering, and if thought fit agreeing to, a scheme of arrangement (with or without modification) proposed to be made between apn and them ("the scheme").
the terms of the scheme are contained in a document that is annexure b to a "scheme booklet".
