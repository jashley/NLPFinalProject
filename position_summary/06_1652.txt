1 in this proceeding the australian competition and consumer commission ('the accc') sought various remedies against the second respondent ('mr carney') for contraventions of the trade practices act 1974 (cth) ('the act').
the relief sought included declarations that mr carney had knowingly been concerned in contraventions of ss 46 and 47 of the act and the imposition of pecuniary penalties for contraventions of those provisions.
