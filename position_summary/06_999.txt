mr and mrs mcclymont engaged wright designed pty limited under two contracts to do work in a bathroom, an en suite, a kitchen and a laundry in their home unit at killara in august 2001.
mr and mrs mcclymont agreed that their property was charged in favour of wright designed as equitable mortgagee to secure payment of all amounts to be paid by them under the contract to the extent that a court or a tribunal had made an order for that payment (cl 23).
