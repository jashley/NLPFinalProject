1 this is an appeal from an order of a federal magistrate made on 22 february 2008 dismissing an application for judicial review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal (the tribunal) made on 25 january 2007 and handed down on 7 february 2007.
the tribunal had affirmed a decision of a delegate of the minister for immigration and multicultural affairs (the first respondent) to refuse to grant a protection (class xa) visa to the appellant.
