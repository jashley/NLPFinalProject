(revised from the transcript) 1 this is an application to admit fresh evidence on the hearing of this appeal.
the evidence consists of an affidavit of the appellant sworn 10 april 2006 in which he asserts that after his previous migration agent, mr jivani had received a notification that a hearing before the refugee review tribunal ('the tribunal') had been fixed for 29 april 2003, mr jivani telephoned him on about 27 april 2003 and told him that if he went to the hearing it would not turn out favourably for him and for that reason mr jivani would not be going and instead was going to go to india and could no longer be his agent.
