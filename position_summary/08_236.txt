introduction 1 the applicant ('australia post') has appealed against a decision of the administrative appeals tribunal ('the tribunal') dated 1 march 2007.
the tribunal's decision was to set aside two reviewable decisions made by australia post under s 62 of the safety, rehabilitation and compensation act 1988 (cth) ('the src act') in which australia post had determined that it was not liable to pay mr sellick compensation for symptoms relating to injuries allegedly sustained while at work.
