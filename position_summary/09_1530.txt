appeal this is an appeal from the judgment of a federal magistrate delivered 7 july 2009.
the federal magistrate dismissed the appellant's application for judicial review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal (tribunal) handed down 21 february 2008, by which the tribunal affirmed a decision of a delegate of the minister for immigration and citizenship (minister) not to grant the appellant a protection (class xa) visa.
