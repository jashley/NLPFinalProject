1 there is before the court an application for the granting of an interlocutory injunction restraining the respondent, the victorian roads corporation ("vicroads"), from offering what are known as v-series number plates for sale.
the v-series number plates are identified in a brochure advertising a premium plate auction which is scheduled to take place tomorrow in south yarra.
