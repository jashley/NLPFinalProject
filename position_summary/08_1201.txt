1 presently before the court are three notices of motion , namely: (a) a notice of motion filed by the applicants dated 4 july 2008 seeking orders for discovery and orders in respect to documents and materials previously seized in accordance with a search order first made pursuant to o 25b of the federal court rules in december 2007 and relevantly varied in april 2008; (b) a notice of motion filed by the respondents dated 15 july 2008 also seeking orders in respect to those seized documents and materials, including an order that the documents seized be returned, other than those referred to in the applicants' amended statement of claim; and (c) a notice of motion of a non-party, citic pacific mining management pty ltd (" citic pacific "), seeking the suspension of the april 2008 orders.
citic pacific is the management company for a project involving the mining and processing of magnetite iron ore at cape preston in western australia's pilbara region.
