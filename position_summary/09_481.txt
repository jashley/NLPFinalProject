background this is an appeal against the severity of sentences imposed on the appellant, interville, for 20 offences of copyright infringement under section 132aj(1) of the copyright act 1968 (cth).
that section provides that a person commits an offence if the person possesses an article with the intention of, inter alia , offering or exposing it for sale or hire to obtain a commercial advantage or profit.
