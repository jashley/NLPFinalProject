introduction 1 mr allan, ms simpson and ms bugg ("the applicants") are members of the wiradjuri people of the bathurst/lithgow/mudgee region of new south wales.
the wiradjuri people are currently pursuing an application for the determination of native title under s 61(1) of the native title act 1993 ("the act ").
