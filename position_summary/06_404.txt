nature of the claim 1 these proceedings, d6033 of 2001, are a consolidation of native title determination applications under the native title act 1993 (cth) (the nt act), filed by three different groups of applicants in relation to land and waters in and around darwin in the northern territory.
the applications were filed between 1994 and 2001.
