buchanan j: phosphate mining on christmas island 1 according to evidence given in the present case by lai ah hong, the managing director of the applicant, phosphate resources limited ('prl'), phosphate mining began on christmas island over 100 years ago, in the late 1890s.
it was a business operation of the clunies ross and murray families.
