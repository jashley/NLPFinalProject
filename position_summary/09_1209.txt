introduction all taxi-cabs in victoria must be licensed and operated in accordance with the terms and conditions of an issued licence: ss 86 , 87 , 139 , 144 and 158 of the transport act 1983 (vic) (the transport act ) (all references to the transport act are as it was in force at the time of the relevant events).
in victoria, the department of transport (victoria) (the dot ) (through the victorian taxi directorate (the vtd )) administers taxi-cab licences: ss 140 , 143 , 143a and 2 (1) of the transport act .
