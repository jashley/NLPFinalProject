the appellant, a minor, now aged 11, appeals from the decision of the federal magistrates court of australia published on 30 june 2008 ( szlwa v minister for immigration &amp; anor [2008] fmca 952) dismissing an application for review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ("the tribunal") of 20 november 2007 affirming a decision of the minister's delegate not to grant the appellant a protection visa.
background to the application for a protection visa the appellant arrived in australia with her mother on 3 february 2001.
