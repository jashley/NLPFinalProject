introduction "the bill express payment system" was an electronic distribution, promotion, sales and bill payment network made up of various brands including dialtime, bill express, bopo, xip media and mobile eft.
the system allowed consumers to pay various accounts and bills and to purchase products and services such as mobile phone credit at participating newsagents and merchants.
