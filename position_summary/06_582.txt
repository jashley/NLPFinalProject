introduction 1 on 23 december 2005, mcinnis fm ordered the respondents to deliver up to the applicant, or cause to be so delivered up, certain property (the mandatory injunction).
the mandatory injunction was granted after a hearing ex parte and upon the applicant giving an undertaking as to damages (the undertaking).
