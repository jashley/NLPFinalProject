his honour 1 this is an appeal from a decision of the federal magistrates court adverse to the appellant, arising out of a decision of the refugee review tribunal (the tribunal).
the tribunal rejected the appellant's application under s 65 of the migration act 1958 (cth) (the act) for a protection (class xa) visa.
