introduction 1 these proceedings concern alleged contraventions of certain provisions of the workplace relations act 1996 (cth) ('the act') said to have been committed in the illawarra region of new south wales on monday 19 january 2004, tuesday 17 february 2004 and wednesday 18 february 2004.
most of the contraventions are said to have occurred at a building site located in woodhill street, fairy meadow ('the fairy meadow site').
