this is an application for a consent determination of native title rights and interests held by the ngurrara people in respect of land and waters within the kimberley region of western australia that has been designated "determination area a".
the parties have agreed to deal with areas of exclusive possession in determination area a separately from reserve, and former reserve, areas which will be dealt with in determination area b.
