introduction 1 by an application filed on 29 june 2006 the australian securities and investments commission (asic) applied for the winding up of thirteen companies in the westpoint property and finance group.
the application was based upon the failure by the companies to comply with statutory demands issued by the receivers and managers of westpoint corporation ltd (westpoint corporation) in respect of the amounts of loans said to be owing by each of them to that company.
