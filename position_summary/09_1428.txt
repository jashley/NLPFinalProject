the appellant, mr slaveski, appeals from a judgment of burchardt fm of the federal magistrates court of australia published on 6 may 2009.
in the judgment below, his honour dismissed mr slaveski's application to set aside a bankruptcy notice served on him on behalf of the respondent company; see slaveski v rotstein [2009] fmca 443.
