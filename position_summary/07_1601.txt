relevant statutory framework 1 under the wheat marketing act 1989 (cth) (the act), the consent of the wheat export authority (the authority) is required for the export of wheat from australia.
in certain circumstances, the minister for agriculture, fisheries and forestry (the minister) must first agree to the giving of such consent by the authority.
