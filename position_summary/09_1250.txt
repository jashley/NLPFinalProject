appeal the appellant appeals from a decision of the federal magistrate made on 28 august 2009 dismissing an application for judicial review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal (tribunal) handed down on 4 december 2008.
the tribunal affirmed the decision of a delegate of the first respondent (minister) on 13 december 2004 to refuse to grant the appellant a protection (class xa) visa.
