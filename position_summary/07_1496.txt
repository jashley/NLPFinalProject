introduction 1 on 6 june 2007, the registrar of aboriginal corporations issued a notice to billabong aboriginal corporation under s 71 of the aboriginal councils and associations act 1976 (cth) ('the act') calling upon billabong to show cause by 22 june 2007 why an administrator should not be appointed.
the date by which billabong is to respond to the notice has been extended by agreement between the parties.
