introduction 1 the administrative appeals tribunal, on an application made by ms debra barry, reviewed three determinations made under the safety, rehabilitation and compensation act 1988 (cth) ('the src act') concerning the liability of australian postal corporation to pay compensation to ms barry following an incident in which ms barry hurt her back while delivering mail.
in this proceeding the corporation challenges the decision of the tribunal on review of one of those determinations.
