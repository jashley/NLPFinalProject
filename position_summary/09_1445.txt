introduction on 27 march 2009, the primary judge delivered judgment in relation to an appeal by television food network, g.p ("television") against a decision of a delegate of the registrar of trade marks dismissing television's opposition to the application by food channel network pty ltd ("channel") to register trade mark no 967804 ("the trade mark"): [2009] fca 271.
the primary judge found in television's favour and ordered that the registration of the trade mark be refused.
