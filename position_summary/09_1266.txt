a deputy commissioner of taxation (deputy commissioner) has applied under the corporations act 2001 (cth) (corporations act) for an order for the winding up of the defendant company, bedroff pty ltd acn 079 158 955 (bedroff).
the basis for the making of the winding up application was a failure on the part of bedroff to comply with a statutory demand served upon it.
