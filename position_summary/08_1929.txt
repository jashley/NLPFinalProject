introduction 1 the applicant ('the land council') seeks a declaration that no native title exists in land at boat harbour in the local government area of port stephens, parish of tomaree, county of gloucester, state of new south wales, being lot 576 in deposited plan 48823 ('the land' or 'lot 576').
this is a non-claimant application, commenced on 31 december 2004, for an approved determination of native title pursuant to s 13 of the native title act 1993 (cth) ('the act').
