introduction 1 on 10 june 2005 i published reasons for judgment finding that native title can be recognised over the traditional lands of the bardi and jawi people on the dampier peninsular and in the inter-tidal zone and adjacent reefs --- sampi v state of western australia [2005] fca 777.
on 4 november 2005 i published a draft determination to give effect to those reasons and to deal with some other issues outstanding from the primary judgment --- sampi v state of western australia (no 2) [2005] fca 1567.
