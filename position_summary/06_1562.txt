his honour: 1 this is an appeal from a decision of the federal magistrates court which dismissed the appellants' application for judicial review of a decision of the migration review tribunal ('tribunal').
the tribunal had affirmed a decision by a delegate of the respondent minister to refuse to grant the appellants a business visa under the migration act 1958 (cth) ('the act').
