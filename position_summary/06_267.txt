outline of the context to the principal proceedings and to the commissioner's application for summary judgment 1 there is pending before the court in its original jurisdiction four principal proceedings brought variously by the taxpayer applicants pursuant to part ivc of the taxation administration act 1953 (cth) ('the administration act'), whereby there is sought to be set aside determinations by the respondent commissioner of taxation ('the commissioner') of objections lodged by the taxpayer applicants against assessments to income tax for the years of income ended 30 june 1991, 1993 and 1994, being questions said by the taxpayer applicants as 'never previously... in issue'.
the taxpayer applicants are respectively identified in these reasons as 'ief' (for iel finance limited), 'qth' (for queensland trading &amp; holding company limited) and 'spassked' (for spassked pty limited).
