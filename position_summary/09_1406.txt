this is an appeal from an order made in the federal magistrates court dismissing the appellants' application pursuant to s 39b of the judiciary act 1903 (cth) and part 8 division 2 of the migration act 1958 (cth) (the migration act ) for judicial review of a decision of the migration review tribunal (the tribunal) dated 7 may 2009 and handed down on 8 may 2009: li tian &amp; ors v minister for immigration &amp; anor [2009] fmca 930.
the appellants are all citizens of the peoples republic of china.
