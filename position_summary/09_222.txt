background and short synopsis of contentions in this proceeding the applicant uniline australia ltd ("uniline") seeks, under s 128(1) of the patents act 1990 (cth) ("the patents act "), a declaration that threats of patent infringement proceedings made by the respondent sbriggs pty ltd ("sbriggs") are unjustifiable; an injunction restraining sbriggs from making further threats of such proceedings; and damages arising out of such threats.
