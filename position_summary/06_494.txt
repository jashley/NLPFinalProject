introduction 1 this is an appeal from a decision of federal magistrate nicholls, given on 8 november 2005, dismissing an application for review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ("the tribunal") handed down on 4 november 2003.
the tribunal affirmed a decision of a delegate of the minister not to grant the applicant a protection visa.
