introduction 1 pursuant to subs 44(1) of the administrative appeals tribunal act 1975 (cth) ("the aat act "), paul and emilse elliott ("the elliotts") appealed against a decision of the administrative appeals tribunal ("the tribunal").
the tribunal's decision, which was given on 15 november 2006, set aside a decision of the social security appeals tribunal ("ssat") of 7 october 2005.
