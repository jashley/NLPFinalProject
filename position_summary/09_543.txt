facts this is an appeal against a decision of a federal magistrate of 19 november 2008 dismissing an application for judicial review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ("the tribunal") delivered 14 february 2008.
the tribunal affirmed the decision of a delegate of the minister for immigration and citizenship to refuse to grant a protection visa to the appellants.
