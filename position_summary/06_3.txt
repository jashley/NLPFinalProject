introduction 1 this is an appeal from a judgment of the federal magistrates court on 9 march 2005 dismissing the appellant's challenge to a decision of the refugee review tribunal ('the tribunal').
the tribunal's decision, handed down on 27 july 2004, affirmed the decision of a delegate of the respondent minister to refuse to grant the appellant a protection visa.
