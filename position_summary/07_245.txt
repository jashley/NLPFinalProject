1 the respondent brings a notice of motion seeking that the applicant's appeal dated 12 september 2006 be dismissed.
in this respect the respondent relies upon the power of the court in s 31a(2) of the federal court of australia act 1976 (cth) to give judgment for one party against another in relation to the whole of a proceeding if (b) 'the court is satisfied that the other party has no reasonable prospect of successfully prosecuting the proceeding ...'.
