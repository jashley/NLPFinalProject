background 1 this is an appeal against a judgment of a federal magistrate delivered on 23 november 2007 dismissing an application for judicial review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ("the tribunal") dated 23 august 2006 and handed down on 24 august 2006.
the tribunal had affirmed a decision of a delegate of the department of immigration and multicultural affairs (as it was then known), to refuse to grant protection visas to the appellants.
