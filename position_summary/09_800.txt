introduction this is an application for a determination of native title in respect of an area of approximately 143.9 hectares ('the claim area') comprising various allotments in the locality of the town of elliott.
the application was filed with the court on 6 july 2001 and registered with the national native title tribunal ('the nntt') on 3 august 2001.
