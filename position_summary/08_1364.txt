introduction 1 this matter arises from the sale of two citrus orchards and a packing shed in southeast queensland, near the towns of bundaberg and tiaro, in 2005, by the first respondent to the first applicant.
the primary proceedings involve a claim by the applicants, seeking relief against the respondents in respect of conduct in breach of ss 51a, 52 , and 53a trade practices act 1974 (cth) ("the act ").
