the nature of the two proceedings these two proceedings arise out of actions of the commonwealth government and the northern territory government in their implementation of the respective government policies in relation to the land colloquially known as the alice springs town camps.
they arise out of concerns about the means by which the commonwealth wishes to improve the living conditions in the alice springs town camps.
