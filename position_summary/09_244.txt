on 3 february 2004, federal magistrate coker made a sequestration order against the appellant, ms khadpekar, on a creditor's petition.
the creditor's petition was founded upon a judgment of the magistrates court in cairns, queensland, in favour of the creditor, the kowanyama aboriginal council.
