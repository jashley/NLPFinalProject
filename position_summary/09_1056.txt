the appellants claim to be bangladeshi homosexuals, who would face a real risk of persecution should they be returned to bangladesh.
after long and extensive litigation, the refugee review tribunal (the tribunal), on the third occasion it had to consider the claims of the appellants, found that the appellants had fabricated their claims, and that they were not truthful or credible witnesses.
