introduction 1 in 2000 the victorian women lawyers' association inc (vwl), an incorporated not for profit association, applied to the commissioner of taxation (the commissioner) for a private ruling in respect of the years ended 31 december 1996 and successive years up to and including that ended 31 december 2000.
it sought a ruling that it was exempt from any obligation to pay income tax on the basis that it is a charitable institution or an association established for community service purposes.
