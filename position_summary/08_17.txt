1 qud 46 of 2000 is an appeal pursuant to s 69(7) of the patents act 1990 (cth) (the old act) from the decision of a delegate of the commissioner of patents (the commissioner) delivered on 19 may 2000.
delegate bruhn refused to grant an extension of petty patent no 662284 (pp2) on the grounds that the applicant was not entitled to the grant of pp2.
