application by an application filed on 3 april 2009 the applicant, suntory (aust) pty ltd (suntory), seeks declarations and related orders in respect of certain excise duties.
suntory claims that it is not liable for and it is unlawful for the respondent, the commissioner of taxation (the commissioner), to collect these excise duties.
