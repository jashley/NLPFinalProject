1 this is an appeal from a decision of federal magistrate driver delivered on 21 june 2006 dismissing an application for review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ('the tribunal') made on 14 november 2005 and handed down on 6 december 2005.
the tribunal affirmed a decision of a delegate of the minister for immigration and multicultural and indigenous affairs, as the minister's title then was, refusing an application for a protection (class xa) visa ('a protection visa') by the applicant.
