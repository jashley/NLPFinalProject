introduction the plaintiff, christopher mel chamberlain (mr chamberlain), is the liquidator of hardy bros (earthmoving) pty limited (in liq) (the company).
mr chamberlain entered into two agreements with the second defendant, the deputy commissioner of taxation (dct), under which mr chamberlain was funded and indemnified against any adverse costs order by the dct, in order to enable mr chamberlain to pursue proceeding 6867 of 2004 in the equity division of the supreme court of new south wales (the transactions proceeding).
