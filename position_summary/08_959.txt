introduction and grounds of appeal 1 this is an appeal from orders of the federal magistrates court made on 11 july 2007 dismissing an application for review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ('tribunal'): szjrw &amp; anor v minister for immigration &amp; anor [2007] fmca 1099.
on 19 october 2006, the tribunal published its decision to affirm a decision of the delegate of the first respondent ('the minister') to refuse to grant each appellant a protection visa under the provisions of the migration act 1958 (cth) ('the act').
