background 1 this is an appeal from a judgment of the federal magistrates court given on 27 october 2006 dismissing the appellant's application for an order that the respondents show cause as to why, amongst other things, a decision of the refugee review tribunal ("the tribunal") should not be quashed.
the tribunal affirmed a decision of the first respondent's delegate not to grant the appellant a protection visa.
