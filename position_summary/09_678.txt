the nature and history of the proceeding in a judgment delivered on 4 october 2007, rural export &amp; trading (wa) pty ltd v hahnheuser [2007] fca 1535 (2007) 243 alr 356 , i dismissed the application of both applicants in this proceeding.
each of the applicants claimed relief of a declaratory and injunctive nature, as well as damages, in respect of an alleged contravention by the respondent, mr hahnheuser, of s 45db of the trade practices act 1974 (cth) ("the trade practices act ").
