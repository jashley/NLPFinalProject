1 the respondent, the commonwealth bank of australia, applies to the court to be relieved from the undertaking it gave to the court on 2 june 2008.
the terms of the undertaking were as follows: the respondent, by its counsel, undertakes to the court that, until further order: i) it will not dismiss the applicant from her employment; and ii) it will continue to pay to the applicant her usual salary without requiring her to perform duties or offering to provide her with duties to perform.
