introduction 1 the plaintiff, nusep ltd ("nusep"), has applied to have shares and options quoted on the australian stock exchange ("asx") (i will use "asx" to refer both to asx limited and to the market that it operates).
nusep issued a prospectus for the issue of the shares and options on 14 december 2006 in which it stated that it would apply within seven days thereafter for such quotation.
