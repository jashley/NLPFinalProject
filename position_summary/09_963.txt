introduction the applicant (the minister) seeks an extension of time in which to appeal from a decision of a federal magistrate quashing a decision of the sixth respondent (the tribunal) that it did not have jurisdiction to entertain an application for review of a decision of the minister's delegate to refuse the first respondent's application for a temporary business (class uc) independent executive subclass 457 visa.
the tribunal decided that valid notification of the delegate's decision had occurred on 16 august 2007, with the result that the prescribed period within which an application could validly be made to the tribunal was 6 september 2007.
