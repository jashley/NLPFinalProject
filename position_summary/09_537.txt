the appellants appeal from the decision of federal magistrate nicholls delivered on 25 august 2008 which dismissed an application for judicial review of a decision of the refugee review tribunal ('the tribunal') handed down on 20 november 2007.
the tribunal had affirmed a decision of a delegate of the minister for immigration and citizenship ('the minister') to refuse to grant a protection (class xa) visa to the appellants.
