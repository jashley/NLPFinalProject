on 15 august 2007, the applicant was granted a subclass 573 higher education sector visa under the migration act 1958 (cth) ( the act ).
this proceeding concerns a decision made on 4 february 2009 by the second respondent, the migration review tribunal ( the tribunal ), affirming a decision by a delegate of the first respondent, the minister for immigration and citizenship ( the migration minister ), to cancel the applicant's visa.
