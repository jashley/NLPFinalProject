introduction this is an appeal from a decision of the commissioner of taxation of the commonwealth of australia (the 'commissioner') disallowing an objection of leaseplan australia limited ('leaseplan') to its gst assessment for the tax period 1 november 2006 to 30 november 2006 ('the relevant period').
