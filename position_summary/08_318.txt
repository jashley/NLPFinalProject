the questions framed by the appeal 1 by this application made in the court's original jurisdiction pursuant to s 44 of the administrative appeals tribunal act 1975 (cth) (the 'aat act'), mr patrick mccutcheon and mrs carolyn mccutcheon appeal from a decision of the administrative appeals tribunal ('the tribunal') by which the tribunal decided two things concerning the taxpayers.
first, the tribunal affirmed an objection decision of the respondent (the 'commissioner') to disallow an objection made by mrs carolyn mccutcheon to an amended assessment issued to her on 16 april 2004 for the income year ending 30 june 1998.
