edmonds j: introduction 1 this is an appeal, pursuant to s 44 of the administration appeals tribunal act 1975 (cth) ('the aat act'), from a decision of the administrative appeals tribunal constituted by mr p j lindsay, senior member ('the tribunal'), given on 18 january 2005.
2 the tribunal's decision was recorded in the following terms: 'the tribunal affirms the decision under review in relation to the inclusion of the sum of $1,228,500 in the applicant's assessable income for the year ended 30 june 1988, but in relation to penalty remits the matter to the respondent with the direction that the assessment for that income year be amended by remitting additional tax in full.
