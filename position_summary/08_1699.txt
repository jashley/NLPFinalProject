introduction 1 each applicant 'appeals' under s 44 of the administrative appeals tribunal act 1975 (cth) (the aat act) from decisions of a senior member of the administrative appeals tribunal (the tribunal) given on 30 april 2007.
the tribunal affirmed the decisions of the commissioner of taxation (the commissioner) to disallow the applicants' objections to the amended assessments of income tax that were issued to them.
