the case has been advanced on the basis that an injunction should issue to maintain the status quo and, as i understand it, additionally or alternatively to maintain the rights of the applicant, being a complainant.
on what has been put, i do not consider that the status quo as identified by the applicant, namely his remaining in australia, is the status quo of the type referred to in s 46pp of the act .
