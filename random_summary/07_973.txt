section 722 is headed " application money to be held on trust " and provides: "(1) if a person offers securities for issue or sale under a disclosure document, the person must hold: (a) all application money received from people applying for securities under the disclosure document; and (b) all other money paid by them on account of the securities before they are issued or transferred; in trust under this section for the applicants until: (c) the securities are issued or transferred; or (d) the money is returned to the applicants.
the opening date was 12 december 2003 and the closing date was 30 january 2004.
(2) if the application money needs to be returned to an applicant, the person must return the money as soon as practicable.
