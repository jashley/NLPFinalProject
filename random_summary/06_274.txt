chapter 6c deals with information about ownership of listed companies .
44 under s 671b, which is in chapter 6c, a person must give certain information to a listed company, if: &#8226; the person begins to have, or ceases to have, a substantial holding in the company; &#8226; the person has a substantial holding in the company and there is a movement of at least 1 per cent in the person's holding; or &#8226; the person makes a takeover bid for securities of the company.
as a noun the word means the act or power of controlling, regulation, domination, command, check or restraint or something that serves to control: see macquarie dictionary (4 th ed).
