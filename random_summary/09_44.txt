mr mccann is not seeking to conduct a dress rehearsal of a possible cross-examination of mr mason.
mr mason, who is a director of caveat, says it is "most likely" he will be called by caveat to give evidence at the trial of the action.
on the other hand, a liquidator is "not entitled to conduct a dress rehearsal of the cross-examination in an action or to seek to damage the opposing parties case by attacking the credibility of that party's witness": sent at 320; see also re hugh j roberts pty ltd (1970) 91 wn (nsw) 537.
