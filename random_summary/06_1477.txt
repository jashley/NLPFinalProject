in vbab v minister for immigration and multicultural and indigenous affairs [2002] fca 804 ; (2002) 121 fcr 100, the appellant was misled by statements of the tribunal.
the full court confirmed the finding of the federal magistrate that where there was no effective consent submitted to the tribunal by an appellant concerning the hearing, the appellant was not bound by the conduct of the agent.
