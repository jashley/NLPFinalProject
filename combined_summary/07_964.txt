introduction 1 on 30 april 2007 a judge of the court (stone j) pronounced an order dismissing mr murdaca's appeal from a judgment of the federal magistrates court.
the judgment of the federal magistrates court had dismissed an application for review of a decision of a registrar to make a sequestration order against mr murdaca's estate.
