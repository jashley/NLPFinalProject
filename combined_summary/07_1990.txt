introduction 1 audi australia pty ltd ("audi") is the australian importer and wholesaler of audi motor vehicles to audi dealers located throughout australia.
this proceeding concerns an advertising campaign conducted by audi in relation to the audi q7 series of motor vehicles, including the audi q7 3.6 se motor vehicle.
