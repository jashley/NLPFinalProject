application for receivers to make provision for funding of opposition to winding up application introduction 1 on 30 march 2006 oren zohar, mark anthony korda and david john winterbottom were appointed as receivers and managers of forestview nominees pty ltd (forestview) under a charge created on 8 december 1998.
on 28 june 2006 the australian securities and investments commission (asic) commenced winding up proceedings against forestview.
a director of forestview, mr norman phillip carey, obtained leave to defend the proceedings in the name of the company.
