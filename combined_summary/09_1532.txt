atp 259p covers land falling within the claimed land.
the first respondent, santos limited (santos) and the third respondent, delhi petroleum pty ltd (delhi petroleum) are the holders of an authority to prospect (atp), atp 259p, issued under the petroleum act 1923 ( qld) (petroleum act).
the applicants, on behalf of the wongkumara people, are the "registered native title claimant" under the native title act 1993 (cth) (nta) in respect of land in south-west queensland and north-west new south wales (the claimed land).
