this judgment concerns the sentence to be imposed on the defendant, paul john rana.
on 22 december 2007, paul rana in proceeding number vid 573 of 2007 pleaded guilty to refusing or failing to comply with a notice issued to him under s 155(1) of the trade practices act 1974 (cth) (the act) contrary to s 155(5)(a) of the act, and in proceeding number vid 571 of 2007 of aiding, abetting, counselling or procuring the commission of an offence contrary to s 155(5)(a) of the act committed by nuera investments pty ltd, namely, the refusal or failure by nuera investments pty ltd to comply with a notice issued to it under s 155(1) of the act.
paul rana should be given credit for the time spent in custody (s 16e(3)).
