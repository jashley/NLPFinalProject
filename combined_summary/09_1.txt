introduction in this proceeding the applicants sought the court's confirmation of a scheme (scheme) for the transfer of part of the life insurance business of the first applicant, the national mutual life association of australasia ltd (nmla), to the second applicant, challenger life no 2 limited.
background both nmla and challenger are registered life insurance companies.
