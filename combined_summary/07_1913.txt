introduction 1 the applicant, mr lawrence rivera, is a citizen of the united states of america who was, at the time of the matters which are the subject of the complaint to the human rights and equal opportunity commission (the commission), in detention in the silverwater metropolitan reception and remand centre under the extradition act 1988 (cth).
mr rivera remains in extradition detention but he is now in the parklea correctional centre pending extradition to the united states.
