introduction the applicant, icm agriculture pty limited (icm), commenced this proceeding on 5 june 2009.
icm applies for a sequestration order under s 43 of the bankruptcy act 1966 (cth) (bankruptcy act) against the estate of the respondent, daryl william young (mr young).
the act of bankruptcy on which icm relies is that execution was issued against mr young under process of a court and was returned unsatisfied.
