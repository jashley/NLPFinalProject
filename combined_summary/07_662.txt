background 2 between 4 and 10 november 2004, members of the australian defence force ("adf"), including trooper lawrence, participating in the field phase of a leaders training course known as the subject 1 for corporal course ("corporal course"), being conducted at the mount bundey training area ("mount bundey") in the northern territory, experienced environmental circumstances giving rise to a risk of heat injury, including heat stroke.
his honour: 1 this case concerns what level of financial "penalty", up to a relatively low limit, it is appropriate for the court to impose on the commonwealth for admitted breaches of the occupational health and safety (commonwealth employment) act 1991 (cth) ("ohs act") by the commonwealth as the deemed employer of a soldier, trooper lawrence, who died at the age of 25 in november 1994 in the northern territory as a result of heat stress sustained while on an army promotional training course.
