(first court hearing) introduction 1 in these two proceedings publishing and broadcasting limited (pbl) and crown limited (crown) respectively seek orders under s 411 of the corporations act 2001 (cth) (the act).
each proceeding relates to a scheme of arrangement between the company and its members --- in pbl's case, the pbl scheme and in crown's case, the demerger scheme.
