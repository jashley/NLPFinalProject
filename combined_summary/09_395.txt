the defendants, narnia investments pty ltd ("narnia") and simon charles clarke have pleaded guilty to charges laid against them by the prosecutor, australian competition and consumer commission ("accc").
narnia has pleaded guilty to knowingly furnishing false information contrary to s 155(5)(b) of the trade practices act 1974 (cth) ("the act").
