introduction and background wyeth is a pharmaceutical company incorporated in delaware, usa.
until recently, it held a patent for the compound venlafaxine hydrochloride, which is the active ingredient in an anti-depressant medicine marketed under the name efexor-xr.
although the compound patent has expired, wyeth continues to hold a method patent for venlafaxine in relation to the blood plasma concentration of that ingredient in the period of about 4 to 8 hours after administration of the drug.
