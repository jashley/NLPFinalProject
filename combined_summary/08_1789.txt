3 before the tribunal, the appellant claimed that she was a member of the thai yai ethnic group in thailand.
further, whether or not the thai yai are persecuted in the fashion asserted by the appellant was irrelevant unless the tribunal had accepted the applicant's personal claims to fear persecution, and to fear that persecution by reason of her thai yai ethnicity.
