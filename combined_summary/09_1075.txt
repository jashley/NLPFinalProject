allphones retail pty limited ( allphones) has been sued by the australian competition and consumer commission ( the accc ) in three separate actions commenced in the new south wales district registry of this court.
subsequently, allphones retained bdo kendalls corporate finance (nsw-vic) pty limited ( bdo ) to prepare two investigative accountant's reports.
in two of those actions, certain individuals who were executives of allphones at relevant times have also been sued.
