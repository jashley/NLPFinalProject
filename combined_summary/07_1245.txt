1 cadbury schweppes pty ltd brought this proceeding against darrell lea chocolate shops pty ltd alleging passing off and contravention of s 52 of the trade practices act 1974 (cth) by the use of the colour purple in connection with the marketing of chocolate products.
2 in the course of the trial i rejected some opinion evidence tendered on behalf of cadbury about consumer behaviour: cadbury schweppes pty ltd v darrell lea chocolate shops pty ltd (2006) 228 alr 719 (the evidence ruling).
subsequently i gave judgment dismissing cadbury's substantive claim: cadbury schweppes pty ltd v darrell lea chocolate shops pty ltd (no 4) (2006) 229 alr 136 (the trial judgment).
