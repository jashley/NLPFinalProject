this is a proud day for the wik and wik way peoples.
the federal court of australia convenes here at aurukun today on country to make and explain the reasons for making orders under s 87 of the native title act 1993 (cth) ("the act") that take effect as a determination of the traditional native title rights and interests of the wik and wik way peoples in the lands and waters of the determination area over which they assert a continuous exercise of traditional rights and interests by their people prior to and since sovereignty.
the application is brought by anthony kerindun, janine chevathun and allison woolla as applicants on their own behalf and for and on behalf of the members of the wik and wik way native title claim group.
