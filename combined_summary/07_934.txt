the trust is established for the benefit of the gumala people who are comprised of the following three language groups - the bunjima people, the niapiali people and the innawonga people.
the trustee is a company and was appointed as the trustee of a trust known as the gumala foundation trust, which was established pursuant to a trust deed made in 1997.
