the appellant alleged that persecution began during the last election campaign when he was threatened and attacked because he was campaigning for the cdpu candidate and against president kuchma.
in his initial application for a protection visa the appellant claimed to have been a member of the christian-democratic party of ukraine ('the cdpu') since 1997 and that he had actively participated in the political life in the city of ternopil.
