order 11 r 16 provides: 16 embarrassment etc where a pleading: (a) discloses no reasonable cause of action or defence or other case appropriate to the nature of the pleading; (b) has a tendency to cause prejudice, embarrassment or delay in the proceeding; or (c) is otherwise an abuse of the process of the court; the court may at any stage of the proceeding order that the whole or any part of the pleading be struck out.
