introduction 1 the applicant has filed a notice of appeal under s 44 of the administrative appeals tribunal act 1975 (cth) ('the aat act') in respect of the decision of the administrative appeals tribunal ('the tribunal') to affirm the minister's decision to cancel the applicant's business visa under s 134 of the migration act 1958 (cth) ('the migration act ').
a privative clause decision is defined in s 474(2) of the migration act as ' a decision of an administrative character made...under this act...other than a decision referred to in subsection (4) or (5) '.
