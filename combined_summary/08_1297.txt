1 the applicant, logic group tyres pty limited ("logic") seeks relief against the first respondent, tokio marine &amp; nichido fire insurance co limited ("tokio"), as insurer under a contract of insurance formed between logic and the second respondent, mirabell point pty limited ("mirabell").
mirabell is said to have been agent for tokio, meaning that tokio is liable for mirabell's representations and conduct concerning the supply of insurance cover for goods stored in premises said to be owned by mirabell.
