the university sues sirtex medical limited (sirtex), a public company established to develop and market the technology.
it also sues the cancer research institute (cri), a charitable incorporated association, which holds shares in sirtex in consideration of assignments to sirtex of rights in relation to some of the technology which it had acquired from dr gray.
