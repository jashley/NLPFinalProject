introduction 1 in august 2007, the australian securities and investments commission ("asic") commenced an investigation pursuant to s 13 of the australian securities and investments commission act 2001 (cth) ("the asic act ") into the activities of awb limited ("awb"), awb (international) ltd ("awbi") and a number of individuals, arising out of or in connection with the supply of wheat to iraq as part of the united nations oil for food programme.
awb did not seek access to the awb information.
it conducted examinations of employees and former employees of awb and awbi pursuant to s 19 of the asic act and, in addition, obtained signed witness statements voluntarily from other employees and former employees.
