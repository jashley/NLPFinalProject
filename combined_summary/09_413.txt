the documents sought to be inspected by the applicants are the subject of a claim by asic that they are privileged from production and inspection on the ground of public interest immunity ("pii").
i accept that limited disclosure of the identity of an informer, such as to persons within the multiplex organisation, does not destroy or negate asic's claim for protection of the identity of that person who is an informer to it on the ground of pii.
multiplex's notice of motion should be dismissed.
