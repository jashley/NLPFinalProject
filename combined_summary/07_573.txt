1 the applicant ('findlay underwriters') by its amended statement of claim, seeks to recover the amounts of $170,000 and $450,000 together with interest which it alleges was paid by it to the first respondent ('carminco') and the second respondent ('tpm') as a result of misleading and deceptive conduct of those companies and of the third respondent ('mr salfinger') who was a director of carminco and tpm.
the claim against each of carminco and tpm alleges breaches of s 52 of the trade practices act 1974 (cth) ('the act') as well as common law contractual claims.
mr salfinger is claimed to be liable pursuant to s 75b of the act for being knowingly concerned in the breaches of s 52 of the act by carminco and tpm.
