introduction the respondent, pioneer kk, applied for two trade mark registrations: no 427184 for the trade mark on 21 may 1985 ('the first pioneer kk mark') and no 767049 for the trade mark on 9 july 1998 ('the second pioneer kk mark') (together, 'the pioneer kk marks').
pioneer kk has no intention to manufacture or sell computers under the pioneer kk marks.
the goods which pioneer computers wishes to have removed from class 9 of the registrations of the pioneer kk marks ('the removal goods') are: computers; computer peripheral devices; computer keyboards; computer memories; printers for use with computers; data processing apparatus; cd-rom disc drives; computer software; computer operating programs; computer game programs it is not in dispute that the removal goods are within the description of goods for which the first pioneer kk mark is registered.
