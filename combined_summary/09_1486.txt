introduction during june 2004, the toy shed pty ltd ("toy shed") placed an order with playspace playground pty ltd ("playspace") for the supply of the component parts necessary to assemble and install playground equipment in 15 city council parks in darwin in the northern territory.
by september 2005, toy shed had failed to pay playspace the various invoices it had submitted for the supply of this equipment.
as a result, playspace obtained a default judgment against toy shed in the county court of victoria in the total sum of $116,525.81, including costs and interest.
