introduction on 15 july 2008, the appellant was made bankrupt by the sequestration order of registrar luxton ("the sequestration order").
the appellant: does not dispute that the respondent obtained a final order of $81,165.31 against him in the magistrates court of victoria on 14 august 2007; does not dispute that the respondent served on the appellant a bankruptcy notice which, subject to whether the notice was an original or a copy, complied with s 41(2) of the bankruptcy act 1966 (cth) ("the bankruptcy act ") and regs 4.01 and 4.02 of the bankruptcy regulations 1996 (cth) ("the bankruptcy regulations "); did not contend that upon receipt of the bankruptcy notice he was unable to establish unambiguously from the notice itself what judgment or order was relied upon for the claimed debt: commonwealth bank of australia v horvath (jnr) [1999] fca 143 ; (1999) 161 alr 441 ; does not dispute that he failed to comply with the bankruptcy notice served on him within the time specified in the notice.
