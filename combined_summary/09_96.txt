introduction the respondents to these proceedings, sons of gwalia ltd (under external administration) ("sog") and its administrators have filed notices of motion seeking to have each of these three proceedings transferred to the supreme court of western australia.
they are founded upon claims of misleading and deceptive conduct and non-disclosure by sog of its true financial position prior to the appointment of the administrators.
