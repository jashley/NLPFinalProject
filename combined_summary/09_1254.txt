the appellant claimed that he was the district secretary of the pattali makkal katchi (pmk) party and a muslim association known as himayathul islam association (hia) which association was responsible for fund raising and maintenance of a new mosque in his home town of adirampattinam in the state of tamil nadu.
the delegate considered that the appellant would not be at risk of persecution for his claimed political activities and associations with either the pmk or hia.
the tribunal made specific findings about the purported letter from the president of the hia ([72]), the letter from the pmk and the membership card ([78]).
