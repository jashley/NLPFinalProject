on 3 april 2009, his honour magistrate g n calder, sitting in the western australian magistrates court at perth determined, pursuant to s 19(2) of the extradition act 1988 (cth) (the act), that the applicant, vincent thomas o'donoghue, is an eligible person for surrender for extradition to ireland in relation to the extradition offences of obtaining property by false pretences (8 counts) and fraudulent conversion (8 counts).
magistrate calder thereupon committed the applicant to custody pursuant to s 19(9) of the act.
