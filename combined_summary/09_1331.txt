this is an application which is brought by mr bryan kevin hughes, as liquidator of firepower operations pty ltd (in liquidation) under s 596a of the corporations act 2001 (cth) for the issue of an examination summons to mr timothy francis johnston.
in support of the application made under s 596a , the applicant relies upon an affidavit of ms kazi mai lyn klopper which annexes a company search of firepower operations pty ltd. the liquidator of a company in liquidation is an "eligible applicant" for the purposes of s 596a.
