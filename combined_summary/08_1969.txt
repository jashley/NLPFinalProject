introduction these reasons for judgment relate to two motions: a motion by the respondent (resmed) for an order pursuant to o 11 r 16 of the federal court rules (cth) (rules) striking out certain paragraphs of the existing further amended statement of claim (fasoc) or, alternatively, pursuant to s 31a(2) of the federal court of australia act 1976 (cth) (fca act) giving judgment for resmed in relation to the claims the subject of those paragraphs, and a motion by the applicant (the university) for leave to file a second further amended statement of claim in the form annexed to the university's notice of motion (sfasoc).
on 4 july 2008 i gave judgment on a discovery dispute between the parties: see the university of sydney v resmed ltd [2008] fca 1020 (the discovery judgment).
