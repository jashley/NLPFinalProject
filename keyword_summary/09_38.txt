pbh is a non-trading entity whose primary role is to hold the investments in midas australia.
neither midas asia pacific pty ltd nor midas corporation pty ltd is in any form of insolvency administration.
midas australia pty ltd is a wholly-owned subsidiary of midas asia pacific pty ltd, which is, in turn, a wholly-owned subsidiary of midas corporation pty ltd, which is in turn a wholly owned subsidiary of pbh asset management pty ltd. midas australia and pbh were placed into administration on 23 december 2008.
