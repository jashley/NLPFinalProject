policyholder means, in relation to a bic policy, the holder of a bic policy.
claims under such policies are to be made to a&amp;g, instead of bic.
personal information means personal information, as defined in the privacy act 1988 (cth), that has been received by bic or a&amp;g or an officer, employee, agent or contractor of bic or a&amp;g in connection with a bic policy.
