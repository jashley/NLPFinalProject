5 the justiciable controversy between the parties to which mr carantinos's application to this court relates concerns the respondent's entitlement to obtain a sequestration order in respect of mr carantinos's estate.
subject to the provisions of s 32aa(2) , mr carantinos's application to this court is a proceeding that s 32aa(1) provides must not be instituted.
in particular, mr carantinos contends, and the respondent disputes, that by reason of the conduct of the respondent, certain dealings between the parties and unfinished litigation in the court of appeal of new south wales, the respondent should be restrained from seeking a sequestration order against mr carantinos's estate.
