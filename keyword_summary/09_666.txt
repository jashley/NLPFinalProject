the proceeding is concerned with the publication by the respondent, clarion marketing australia pty ltd (clarion), of cards commonly known as "scratch cards" or "scratchies" that are said to be misleading or deceptive.
clarion has promoted its moby planet content service through the publication of a yellow scratch card which was later replaced by a red scratch card.
clarion is a content service provider which provides its services to users of mobile phones through short message services and other means of communication.
