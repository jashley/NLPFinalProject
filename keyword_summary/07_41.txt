ksl was incorporated in australia on 21 august 2000 with all shares in ksl directly owned by kyc.
the business was transferred from telstra to ksl pursuant to an agreement between telstra, ksl and kyc dated 25 october 2000.
a loss transfer agreement was entered into to transfer $27,748,457 in tax losses from kyc to ksl in order to reduce the taxable income of ksl to nil.
