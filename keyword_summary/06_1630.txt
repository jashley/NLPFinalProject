in 2001 and 2002 respectively, mark alan silbermann ('mr silbermann') and john david rich ('mr rich') filed separate proceedings in the supreme court of new south wales against cgu.
that application was dismissed by bergin j on 3 september 2003: silbermann v cgu insurance ltd (2003) 47 acsr 21.
mr street acknowledges that an order has been made staying the rich and silbermann proceedings until the conclusion of the hearing of the civil penalty proceedings brought by asic against mr rich and mr silbermann.
